The HOW of it

Get started with these simple steps

Register

Every new user will register with their email addresses, and a Referee Code gotten from an already existing member. The registration process will validate your given email, and then activate your account.

Bitcoin Address

Being a platform based off of the Secured Bitcoin crypto currency, every participant is required to have a bitcoin address. Your bitcoin address is like your bank account - transactions are directed to it, or originate from it. Various Trusted Bitcoin wallet services exist that offer wallet services - for now, BitDiamond does not host it's own wallet service. With one of these services, you get an address with which your transactions will be made; once gotten, you supply this addresss to the system for verification.

Bit Levels & Leveling Up

The system uses a cycle-system to keep track of user's status. Each cycle is made up of 3 levels, and one recycle level. When a user becomes a part of our system, He is first and formost on Cycle 1, Level 0.
To advance to Cycle 1, Level 1, a fee of 0.11 must be paid to the closest eligible upline (selected automatically). From Cycle 1, Level 1, a fee of 0.1826 is paid to the next eligible upline selected by the system.
Finally, to move to Cycle 1, Level 3, 0.3652 is paid to the given upline. Once paid, the user it recycled to Cycle 2, Level 0, and the process starts over again.

Upline Eligibility

To be eligible for selection by the system to receive donations, a user must be at least one level higher than the person making the donation.
