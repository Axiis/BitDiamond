FAQs

  • General

What is BitDiamond?

BitDiamond is a 2x3 forced matrix that uses ONLY bitcoin for transactions. It's very fast, easy, and sustainable

How does BitDiamond work?

When you join BitDiamond you become a member of a community of people helping each other to quickly acquire wealth by making donations to one another.

How do I join BitDiamond?

Every member has a unique referral link. Contact your referee and he or she will give you his or her referral link. Use the link to sign up.

Is BitDiamond available worldwide?

Yes, BitDiamond is available to anyone with an internet connection, a Bitcoin wallet and the willingness to become a membe.

What pay processors are used?

We only use Bitcoin to make it easy for members to make contributions to other members.

Are there any refunds?

All contributions happen between members and it is up to members to reach an agreement amongst themselves if they allow refunds or not

I don't have a bitcoin wallet where do I go to open a bitcoin wallet?

The only place we highly recommend you go get a Bitcoin wallet is at Block Chain Info

Do we only upgrade to the next tier after 1 calendar month?

You should upgrade to the next tier the moment you are able to so that you don't miss out on contributions coming to you for those tiers.

Are you allowed to upgrade even though your tier is not filled up?

You can upgrade at any time, since there are no subscriptions (renewals) you'll be sure to get the donations that are due you on all tiers.

Is it possible for downlines to upgrade to your upline instead of you if you're not on that tier yet?

BitDiamond is designed to be very fast, so we strongly advise that you upgrade as soon as you can. If you don't you will miss donations for the levels you have not upgraded to if there are members below you on those tiers who upgrade fast.

When will two people be assigned to me? I was under the impression that as each person joins they are assigned under each person and so forth under my tier?

You will have to find your own two people to join you and you can then help them find their 2 people to grow your team. Don't rely or expect spillovers, even though spillovers happen automatically.

Isn't this like a pyramid scheme?

Well, a pyramid scheme means that only the top members of the hierarchy get paid, but that's not the case with BitDiamond. BitDiamond allows every member to earn the same amount by doing the same amount of work. You can receive more contributions than the person who signed you up, that fact makes BitDiamond a fair system.

Do I have to renew each level every month?

No. There are no renewals in BitDiamond. You only pay for a level only once.

Can I use the same IP address to help my team to open accounts for multiple people?

Yes you can.

What happens when I get to level 3?

When you get to level 3 you will receive donations from your level 3 downlines. It is recommended that you cycle out and start from level 1 so you are placed under your original upline. What this means is, you follow your upline for life!

Will I miss a donation as a result of delay of payment confirmation from Blockchain?

When you make a donation and you want it to be quickly confirmed, notify your upline and he or she will manually confirm your payment.
